Robotics Society, NIT Hamirpur

Vision and Moto

To create awareness regarding the role of robots in industries and day to day life
To provide a platform for Robotics Enthusiasts to collaborate and exchange Ideas
To mentor the potential Robotics Enthusiasts to take part in Research Activities
To facilitate students to learn robotics and create robots using their creative skills

Activity Highlights

Nimbus 2k18

Nimbus is the annual technical fest of NIT Hamirpur. In 2k18, we showcased the following projects: Driverless Car, Humanoid Robot, Facial Recognition, Voice recognition, CNC Machine.

Robotics Day 2k19

Celebrated in the memory of our Co-Founder Late Lamyanba Heisnam, we oraganize events and showcase our projects to students.

NImbus 2k19
